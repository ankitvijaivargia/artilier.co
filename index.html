The Atelier

Where vision
becomes material.

ARTILIER is a craft atelier rooted in Delhi, built for the world's most demanding design studios. We translate bold creative visions into custom furniture, material systems, and singular objects — worked by hands that have spent decades learning their craft.

We do not manufacture at scale. We do not maintain a catalogue. We respond to briefs.

What We Work With

Four materials.
Infinite possibilities.

01
Genuine
Leather
Full-grain, vegetable-tanned, and exotic leathers sourced for their character and worked by craftspeople who understand hide at a granular level.
02
Hand-worked
Metal
Brass, bronze, blackened steel, and patinated copper — cast, forged, and finished by metalworkers whose knowledge of temperature and pressure is entirely embodied.
03
Luxury
Textile
Velvet, boucle, hand-woven wool, and silk — selected for pile depth, structural integrity, and the way they hold light differently at every angle of incidence.
04
Stone
& Marble
Rajasthani sandstone, Italian marble, onyx, and travertine — quarried for veining character and worked with abrasives that reveal the depth of the material rather than eliminating it.
The ARTILIER Conviction

The difference between a good object and an extraordinary one lives entirely in the making.

We are not the cheapest option. We are not the fastest option. We are the option for designers who understand that the object they specify will exist in a space for twenty years — and must be exactly right.

How We Work

A brief,
not a catalogue.

01
The Brief
Send us your material board, sketch, and project context. We read it carefully. We ask one or two precise questions. We do not send a form.
02
Material Direction
Within 10 days, we respond with three material-finish directions, an initial sampling cost, and a production timeline. No catalogue. Just a response to your specific vision.
03
Sampling
We produce samples. Three to five rounds is normal. Each round is documented with photographs and notes. Iteration is not inefficiency — it is the process.
04
Production
We make it. We send status reports before they're requested. When something goes wrong, you hear from us first — with a solution attached. Never a surprise at delivery.
